LEGAL INTAKE POLICY
Fictitious Bank Operations (synthetic document for demo)

LEG-1. Contract review intake.
Contract review requests must include counterparty, business sponsor, document
type, target signature date and unusual clauses.

LEG-2. NDA standard path.
Standard mutual NDAs with confidentiality terms up to 3 years can be approved by
Legal Operations.

LEG-3. Non-standard clauses.
Foreign jurisdiction, terms above 3 years, unilateral obligations or unusual data
sharing clauses require Legal Counsel review.

LEG-4. Urgent review.
Urgent reviews can be prioritized when business sponsor and signature deadline are
present, but non-standard clauses still require counsel.
