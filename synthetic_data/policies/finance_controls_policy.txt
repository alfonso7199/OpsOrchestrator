FINANCE OPERATIONS CONTROL POLICY
Fictitious Bank Operations (synthetic document for demo)

FIN-1. Invoice matching.
Invoices must match the purchase order amount, vendor and contract owner before
payment can be released.

FIN-2. Tolerance threshold.
Differences up to 2,000 EUR may be approved by the business owner if budget is
available.

FIN-3. Material exception.
Differences above 2,000 EUR require Finance Controller approval and Procurement
confirmation before payment.

FIN-4. Critical vendor handling.
Critical vendor exceptions may be expedited, but the approval route cannot be
skipped.
