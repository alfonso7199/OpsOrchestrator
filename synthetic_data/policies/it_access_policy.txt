IT ACCESS CONTROL POLICY
Fictitious Bank Operations (synthetic document for demo)

IT-1. Access requests.
Access to operational systems must include requester identity, manager, cost
center, target system, requested role and business justification.

IT-2. Standard role approval.
Standard roles listed in the access catalog may be approved by the line manager
and fulfilled by IT Service Desk.

IT-3. Privileged access.
Privileged or admin roles require Security approval and must be time-bound.

IT-4. Same-day urgency.
Urgent same-day access can be expedited only when manager and cost center are
present and the role is standard.
