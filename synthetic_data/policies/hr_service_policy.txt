HR SERVICE POLICY
Fictitious Bank Operations (synthetic document for demo)

HR-1. Employment certificates.
Employment certificates can be issued by HR Operations after verifying employee
identity, certificate purpose and whether salary disclosure is requested.

HR-2. Salary disclosure.
Certificates including salary require employee consent in writing and must be
delivered to the employee, not directly to a third party.

HR-3. Missing information.
If purpose, delivery language, salary disclosure consent or employee identifier
is missing, HR must request the missing data before issuing the certificate.
